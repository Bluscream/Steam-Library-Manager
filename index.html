Features

Fail-safe

Task Manager and managed tasks are running on a different thread.

So: If an error happens while moving, SLM will stop the process and warn the user without touching to anything.

Fast

SLM is tested and different methods compared to get the fastest results before reaching to the community. Also faster than Steam's itself.

Library Cleaner

You can use SLM to clean junks from your library by moving or removing them.

Customizable

Because every person is unique with their own likings. You can customize most of the functions in SLM as you like.
